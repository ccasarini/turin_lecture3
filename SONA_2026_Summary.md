# Summary: 2026 State of the Nation Address (SONA)
**President Cyril Ramaphosa**
*Date: February 12, 2026*

The 2026 SONA focuses on a "turning point" for South Africa, transitioning from a period of decline toward sustainable growth. Key themes, commitments, and policy announcements include:

## 1. Security & Crime
*   **Gang Violence:** Deployment of the South African National Defence Force (SANDF) to combat gang violence specifically in the Western Cape and Gauteng.
*   **Governance:** Introduction of the **Whistle-Blower Protection Bill** to strengthen anti-corruption efforts.

## 2. Infrastructure & Resource Management
*   **Water Crisis:** Establishment of a **National Water Crisis Committee** chaired by the President.
*   **Investment:** A **R156 billion investment** in water infrastructure to ensure long-term security.

## 3. Economy & Investment
*   **Investment Target:** A new **R2 trillion investment target** to stimulate economic activity.
*   **Green Energy:** A **150% tax deduction** for investments in new energy vehicles (NEVs) to promote a green transition.

## 4. Agriculture & Health
*   **Animal Health:** Classification of foot-and-mouth disease as a **national disaster**, facilitating a mass vaccination campaign for 14 million cattle.
*   **Public Health:** Rollout of **Lenacapavir** for HIV prevention and a commitment to **end child stunting by 2030**.

## 5. Digital & Local Government
*   **Digital ID:** Introduction of a **Digital ID system** to modernize citizen services.
*   **Local Governance:** Implementation of a revised **White Paper on Local Government** to improve service delivery at the municipal level.
